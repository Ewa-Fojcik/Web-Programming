Treasures Hunt

Aim of the game

Collect as many treasures as you can, while avoiding obstacles.
Make sure to strategically plan your movements to maximize the score.
Adapt the set-up to increase the difficulty.
The game ends when all treasures ale collected, your movements are blocked, or when you choose to stop.

Game Rules

* Enter a number between 5 and 8 to place a treasure. The number indicates the value of the treasure.
* Click on a cell and type the letter "o" to place an obstacle.
* Click on a cell and type the letter "h" to place the treasure hunter.

How to move

* Use "a" to move the treasure hunter one cell to the left
* Use "d" to move the treasure hunter one cell to the right
* Use "w" to move the treasure hunter one cell up
* Use "s" to move the treasure hunter one cell down
